How to Use the Weather App

Introduction

Welcome to the Weather App! This guide will help you get the most out of our app, providing step-by-step instructions on how to check the weather, understand the data, and make the best use of the features available.

Step 1: Search for a City

To get started, enter the name of the city you want to check the weather for in the search bar at the top of the page. Press the "Search" button to retrieve the weather data for the specified city.

Step 2: View Current Weather

After searching, the current weather conditions for the city will be displayed. You can see the temperature, feels-like temperature, minimum and maximum temperatures, humidity, wind speed, and more.

Step 3: Check Wind and Cloud Information

The app also provides detailed information about wind speed, wind direction, and cloud coverage. This data helps you understand the current weather conditions more comprehensively.

Step 4: Sunrise and Sunset Times

Additionally, the app shows the sunrise and sunset times for the selected city. This information is useful for planning your day and activities.

Popular Cities

At the bottom of the main page, you can find weather information for some popular cities. This section provides a quick overview of the weather in major cities.

Tips for Using the App

  • Use the search function to find weather data for any city worldwide.
  • Check the wind and cloud information for a better understanding of weather conditions.
  • Look at the sunrise and sunset times to plan your outdoor activities.
  • Refer to the popular cities section for a quick weather update of major locations.
